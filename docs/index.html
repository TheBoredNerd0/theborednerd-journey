📚 Today's Lesson

Compound Interest

The eighth wonder of the world

📖 Concept

Earn returns on your returns — not just on your original money, but on everything you've accumulated so far. The math: A = P × (1 + r)ⁿ · Time is the multiplier. A 25-year-old investing $200/month at 8% average returns has ~$1.1M by age 65. Start at 35 with the same amounts? ~$487K.

🧠 Key Lesson

Volatility is not the same as risk. A crypto dip is just noise if you're investing with a 5–10 year horizon. True risk is: (1) investing money you can't afford to lose, (2) not diversifying, or (3) panic-selling.

🇸🇬 Singapore Context

CPF Special Account earns ~4% p.a. — compound interest working for you automatically. STI ETF (ES3.SI) has returned ~5–7% annually over long periods. Even a 1% difference in fees matters enormously over 30 years.
