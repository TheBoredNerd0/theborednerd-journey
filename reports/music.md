## ⚖️ Legal Corner — CRITICAL UPDATE

**Musixmatch just launched "Sentinel"** — a real-time copyright detection service for AI/UGC content:
- Detects even partial use of copyrighted lyrics "in milliseconds"
- Already integrated with 200,000+ music publishers
- All three majors (UMPG, Sony, Warner Chappell) signed AI licensing deals with Musixmatch in Oct 2025
- **What this means for you:** Platforms are getting serious about filtering AI music. Key risks:
  1. **Sampling recognizable lyrics** — even short phrases can trigger flags
  2. **Trained on copyrighted works** — Lyria 3 outputs are watermarked with SynthID; platforms can detect them
  3. **Nuanced rule:** AI music that's purely instrumental and doesn't mimic specific artists is lower risk
- **Safe play:** Stick to fully original instrumental compositions, avoid any prompt that references specific artists or copyrighted lyric phrases
