## 🛡️ Security

**RoadK1ll — New WebSocket-Based Malware Implant (CVSS 8.5)**

Blackpoint Cyber discovered a new Node.js-based implant during active intrusions. It communicates over custom WebSocket protocol to give attackers persistent, covert access to breached networks — and bypasses perimeter firewalls entirely by making outbound connections from the infected host.

Key detail: **no traditional persistence** (no registry keys, no scheduled tasks) — only runs while the process is alive. That makes it stealthier than typical malware. Watch for anomalous outbound WebSocket connections from Node.js processes on endpoints.
