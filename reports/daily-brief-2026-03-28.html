📚 This Week: DCA

Dollar-Cost Averaging

The simplest wealth-building strategy most people overthink

📖 Concept

Invest a fixed amount at regular intervals regardless of market direction. When prices drop, your S$100 buys more units; when they rise, you buy fewer. Over time, this smooths out your average purchase price. Studies consistently show DCA outperforms trying to time the market for most retail investors.

🧠 Key Lesson This Week

Volatility is not the same as risk. A 3% daily crypto drop is just noise if you're investing with a 5–10 year horizon. True risk is: (1) investing money you can't afford to lose, (2) not diversifying, or (3) panic-selling. A young investor shouldn't fear a 3% dip — they should see it as a buying opportunity.

🎯 Actionable — Start With S$100

Open a Syfe, MooMoo, or Interactive Brokers account and buy a single ETF like CSPX or VWRA. Set up a S$20/week DCA plan and watch how it compounds over 6 months. That's real education no course can teach.
